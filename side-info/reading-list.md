| Book                                                                                             | Kya seekh milti hai / Benefit                                                                                                                                                             |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **The Innovator's Dilemma** — Clayton M. Christensen                                             | Batata hai ki kaise big companies disrupt ho jaati hain aur kaise ek naya competitor unki weakness exploit kar sakta hai. Agar tu chahta hai long-term disruptor bane, yeh essential hai. |
| **Zero to One** — Peter Thiel & Blake Masters                                                    | Business + startup + monopoly mindset ka modern guide. “Unique value”, “competition avoid karo”, “monopoly build karo” — yeh concepts isme hain. Tum jaise bande ke liye perfect.         |
| **Hard Drive: Bill Gates and the Making of the Microsoft Empire** — James Wallace & Jim Erickson | Direct history of Microsoft — kaise Gates ne strategic deals kiye, kya risks uthae. Agar Gates-level story dekhni hai, yeh book padhni chahiye.                                           |
| **The Lean Startup** — Eric Ries                                                                 | Agar tu apni company start karna chahta hai — risk kam karne, iterate karne, product-market fit lene ka system yeh sikhata hai. Strategy + discipline dono.                               |
| **Good to Great** — Jim Collins                                                                  | Business ko long-term sustainable kaise banaate hain, kaise company ko “great” karte hain — structure, leadership, culture ka funda hai.                                                  |

### Order

1. Hard Drive — Microsoft ka origin samajhne ke liye.

2. Zero to One — Future startup / monopoly mindset ke liye.

3. Innovator’s Dilemma — Disruption aur competition ka scientific perspective.

4. Lean Startup — Practical startup building & execution.

5. Good to Great — Company ko strengthen karte rehne ka structure.
